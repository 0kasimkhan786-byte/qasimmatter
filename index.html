Our Story

Born from the Soil of Kannauj

Qasim Attar was born from a simple belief — that the finest fragrances should come from the earth, not a laboratory. We source our attars directly from the heart of Kannauj, Uttar Pradesh — India's perfume capital for over a thousand years.

Unlike mass-market brands, every bottle of Qasim Attar carries the authenticity of traditional deg-bhapka distillation — pure, potent, and free from any synthetic additives or alcohol.

Our promise is simple: what you smell is exactly what nature created.
